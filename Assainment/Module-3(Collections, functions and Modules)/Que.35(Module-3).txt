Que.35-how do user traverse through a dictionary object in python?
Ans.    
        step-1:
                first of all define dictionary, and initialise it's keys
            and values.

        step-2:
                in this step,user provide for loop for this program,because
            print sequentially dictionary's key and values.

        step-3:
                after then,print {key} and {values}.

        
        Ex.
            Sample_Traverse={"Apple":1,"Banana":2,"Cherry":3,"Grapes":4,"Mango":5}

            for key,value in Sample_Traverse:
                    print(f"key:{key},value:{value}")
